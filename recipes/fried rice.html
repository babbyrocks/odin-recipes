Fried Rice

Ingredients

Vegetables and Add-ins

  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped green beans
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs (optional, for egg fried rice)

Rice and Seasoning

  • 3 cups cooked, day-old white rice (preferably jasmine or basmati)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil or butter
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 bouillon cube or 1 teaspoon bouillon powder (optional)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. If using eggs, scramble them lightly and set aside.
  2. Add remaining oil to the pan. Sauté onions and garlic until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add carrots, green beans, peas, and sweet corn. Stir-fry for about 5–7 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Add bell peppers and cook for another 2–3 minutes. Season with curry powder, thyme, bouillon (if using), salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir in cooked rice, breaking up any clumps. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Mix thoroughly to combine all ingredients well.
  6. If using scrambled eggs, add them back to the pan and mix. Continue stir-frying everything for another 3–5 minutes until heated through.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Cook's Note

If using freshly cooked rice, spread it out on a tray and let it cool completely before frying. This helps prevent mushy fried rice.
